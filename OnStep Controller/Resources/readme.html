Initilization: Setting up and Alignment

  1. This App controls through Wi-Fi from your iPhone or iPad. Go to your device's Settings > Wi-Fi > Connect with your Goto’s Wi-Fi.
  2. In the app tap on more button. It will open up a list of settings.
    1. Tap on ’Connection’ that will take you to Connection settings page.
    2. Add IP Address and Port to access point. Wi-Fi connections in access-point mode defaults to address 192.168.0.1
    3. Then just tap ’Save’. This is just one time thing, then app will always remember your IP Address and Port.
  3. At top-right side, on landing screen tap more button and then tap on ’Site Selection’. Add your Site name, Latitude, Longitude, UTC Offset, and then tap Upload. Server should revert with a success message.
  4. On landing screen, tap Initialize/Park. Then tap Date/Time button. This will update time on the Goto server.
  5. Now that OnStep has the correct site information and date/time you can start an Align.
    1. A list of suitable stars will be displayed to choose from.
    2. Pick a bright easily identified star not too close to the NCP/SCP and do a goto.
    3. Depending on your configuration, up to 3 stars may be used.
    4. If the mount is well constructed and polar aligned and OnStep was setup correctly the star should be in a wide field eyepiece of a small telescope or in your finder ’scope.
    5. Use the guide controls to center the star in the eyepiece.
    6. Finally, select "Align" on the direction pad. If more alignment stars are required the list of stars will reappear and this sequence will repeat until done.

FAQ:

  • Two star and three start alignment isn’t working?
    • — Two star and three star align is not supported with Fork mounts in alternate mode.
  • PEC isn’t?
    • — PEC is not supported with AlT/AZM mounts, for rest all mount types it should work.
  • Goto speed is too slow/fast?
    • — You might want to increase/decrease the MaxRate, it’s possible to change from app upto 2x.
  • There’s a bug/crash you’d want to report?
    • — You can always reach me out at my email and OnStep group.

If you have any other question, please take advantage of OnStep’ group.
